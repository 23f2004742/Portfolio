About Me

Who Am I?

I am a student at the Indian Institute of Technology, Madras, pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Data Science and Applications (May 2023 – September 2027). I combine rigorous academics with hands-on entrepreneurial experience, driven by curiosity and the passion to build solutions that create real-world impact.

I am the CEO & Founder of SoApp, a retail automation startup reimagining the shopping experience. At SoApp, I lead all aspects of the business, from product development and market analysis to stakeholder engagement and strategic planning. Our dual NFC and RFID integrated self-checkout solutions address critical retail challenges like long checkout queues, inventory mismanagement, and operational inefficiencies. I'm architecting a scalable platform that combines hardware integration with data-driven decision-making.

Beyond entrepreneurship, I am passionate about community leadership. As Treasurer of Shah Maat (IITM's Chess Club), I manage operations for 500+ members, overseeing financial operations and strategic initiatives. I previously served as Society Affairs Head, coordinating cross-functional teams and major events like the Interhouse Chess Championship during Paradox'24 with 200+ participants.

I am also an author, having published "What to Do in Your Teenage"—a guide designed to help young people build confidence, develop initiative, and unlock their potential during formative years. This work reflects my commitment to knowledge sharing and personal growth.

My expertise spans Data Science, Machine Learning, Full-Stack Development, and Retail Systems Architecture. I combine technical depth with business acumen, applying strategic thinking and data analytics to solve complex problems at the intersection of technology and entrepreneurship.

IFC File Error Detection System

Role: ML Developer

Tech Stack: Machine Learning, Python, Classification Models

Developed an ML pipeline to detect structural and syntactic errors in IFC (Industry Foundation Classes) files used in infrastructure construction. Built a parser for feature extraction, trained classification models (Decision Tree, Random Forest, Gradient Boosting), and implemented an automated validation wrapper. The system provides entity-level error reports and assigns file-level risk scores, enabling teams to prioritize file review by severity.
